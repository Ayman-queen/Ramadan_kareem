About Ramadan

The ninth month of the Islamic calendar

üåô

Fasting

Muslims fast from dawn until sunset, abstaining from food, drink, and worldly pleasures. This spiritual practice purifies the soul and brings us closer to Allah.

Prayer

Special Taraweeh prayers are performed every night. Muslims increase their worship, recite the Quran, and seek forgiveness during this blessed month.

Charity

Giving to those in need is highly encouraged. Zakat and Sadaqah help purify wealth and bring communities together in compassion and generosity.
